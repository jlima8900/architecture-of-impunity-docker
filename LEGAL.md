# Legal Notice & Disclaimer

## Purpose and Nature of This Project

This project is an **academic research compilation** and **public interest journalism initiative** documenting publicly available information about Portugal's judicial and political accountability systems. It is intended for:

- Academic research and analysis
- Journalistic investigation
- Public education and democratic discourse
- Policy analysis and reform advocacy

## Source Attribution

**ALL information presented in this project is derived exclusively from publicly available sources**, including but not limited to:

### Official Government Sources
- Portuguese Parliament (Assembleia da República) - Official records and voting records
- Constitutional Court of Portugal (Tribunal Constitucional) - Published rulings
- DCIAP (Central Department of Investigation and Penal Action) - Public statements
- Portuguese Government Official Gazette (Diário da República)

### International Organizations
- GRECO (Group of States Against Corruption) - Council of Europe evaluation reports
- European Commission - Rule of Law Reports
- Transparency International - Corruption Perception Index
- Eurobarometer - Public opinion surveys
- FATF (Financial Action Task Force) - Evaluation reports

### Court Records and Legal Proceedings
- Publicly available court decisions
- Official indictments made public through legal proceedings
- Published judicial rulings

### Reputable News Media
- Major Portuguese news outlets (Público, Observador, Expresso, RTP, SIC, TVI)
- International news organizations (BBC, Financial Times, Reuters, Bloomberg)
- Investigative journalism consortiums (ICIJ, OCCRP)

**Every factual claim includes source attribution with direct links to original sources.**
